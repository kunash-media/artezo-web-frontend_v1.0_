Shipping & Delivery

At ARTEZO, we are committed to delivering your products safely, securely, and within a reasonable timeframe. This Shipping Policy outlines our processing times, delivery timelines, and shipping practices.

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

1. Order Processing Time

  • All orders are dispatched within 24-48 hours after confirmation of payment.
  • For personalized products, custom nameplates, corporate gifts, trophies, and mementos, processing time may take 2-3 business days, depending on design approval and customization requirements.
  • Orders are not processed on Sundays or public holidays.

2. Shipping & Delivery Timeline

Once dispatched, estimated delivery timelines are:

At Artezo, we ensure your orders are dispatched within 48 hours of confirmation. We deliver across India, and you can expect your order to arrive within 4 to 7 business days after dispatch.

Delivery timelines may vary depending on courier services and location accessibility.

3. Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charges (if applicable) will be calculated and displayed at checkout.
  • We may offer:
    • Free shipping on select products
    • Free shipping above a certain order value
    • Special shipping terms for bulk or corporate orders

4. Order Tracking

You can easily log into your Account to track your order anytime. Our reliable shipping partners, Bluedart, Xpressbees, Delhivery, DTDC, ATS, and other trusted partners, help us facilitate seamless delivery nationwide.

Please get in touch with our customer support team if you have any questions or need further assistance.

5. Packaging

All ARTEZO products are securely packaged to prevent damage during transit. Special care is taken for:

  • Photo Frames
  • Wall Decor
  • Glass components
  • Trophies and Mementos

We prioritize protective packaging to ensure your order arrives in excellent condition.

6. Delivery Delays

While we strive for timely delivery, delays may occur due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Courier disruptions
  • Public holidays
  • Incorrect shipping details provided by the customer

ARTEZO shall not be held liable for delays caused by external courier partners or unforeseen circumstances.

7. Incorrect Address

Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping information. If an incorrect or incomplete address is provided:

  • Delivery may be delayed or returned
  • Additional shipping charges may apply for re-dispatch

8. Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or appears tampered:

  • Please notify us within 24 hours of delivery
  • Share clear photos/videos of the packaging and product
  • We will review the issue and assist accordingly

9. Bulk & Corporate Orders

For large volume corporate orders, shipping timelines and charges may vary based on:

  • Quantity
  • Customization requirements
  • Delivery location

Our team will communicate expected timelines during order confirmation.
