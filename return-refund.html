Return, Refund & Cancellation

At ARTEZO, we strive to ensure that every product delivered meets our quality standards and your expectations. This policy outlines the terms under which returns, replacements, and refunds are processed.

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

1. Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns or replacement requests only under the following circumstances:

  • The product is damaged during transit
  • The product received is defective
  • The wrong product was delivered

To be eligible:

  • The issue must be reported within 24–48 hours of delivery
  • Clear photos and an unboxing video must be shared (showing packaging and product condition)
  • The product must be unused and in its original packaging

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following products are not eligible for return or refund:

  • Personalized or customized products (including nameplates, engraved gifts, custom trophies, etc.)
  • Corporate or bulk orders
  • Products damaged due to misuse, mishandling, or improper installation
  • Minor variations in color/finish due to lighting or screen display

As many of our products are made-to-order or customized, returns are generally limited to manufacturing defects or transit damage only.

3. Replacement Policy

If a product is found to be damaged or defective:

  • We may offer a replacement at no additional cost
  • Replacement processing time: 3–7 business days after approval
  • ARTEZO reserves the right to verify the damage before approving replacement

4. Refund Policy

Refunds (if applicable) will be processed only when:

  • Replacement is not possible
  • The product is out of stock
  • The issue qualifies under our return policy

Refund Timeline:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days after approval
  • Refund will be issued to the original payment method
  • Shipping charges (if any) are non-refundable unless the error was on our part

5. Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled:

  • Within 12–24 hours of placing the order
  • Before the product is shipped
  • Customized or personalized orders cannot be cancelled once production has started

6. Return Shipping

If a return is approved:

  • Customers may be instructed to ship the product back
  • In cases of genuine defect or incorrect delivery, ARTEZO may bear return shipping costs

7. Important Notice for Customized Products

Since ARTEZO specializes in personalized gifts, nameplates, trophies, and custom decor:

  • Customers are requested to carefully review spelling, customization details, and design approvals before confirming orders
  • Once approved and processed, such products cannot be returned or refunded

Our support team will guide you through the process.

9. Policy Updates

ARTEZO reserves the right to modify this Return & Refund Policy at any time without prior notice. Customers are encouraged to review this page periodically.
